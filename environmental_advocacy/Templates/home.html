About EcoDada

In the face of climate change, there exists a huge equity gap between men and women. Traditionally, womenâ€™s roles put them at a disadvantage in trying to deal with the impacts of climate change. Yet, they go unheard in most cases. In general, climate action is our main objective. However, with a huge percentage of its members being ladies, Eco- Dada Youth initiative aims to give a voice and support to all the young girls and women in the community. It also aims to closely work with the children and the community in general to create more environmental awareness.

Thematic Areas

  • Women Empowerment
  • Environmental advocacy and awareness
  • Climate change adaptation and mitigation
  • Waste management

Approach

  • The initiative plans to achieve these by working directly with women from all settings through innovative projects that can help better their livelihoods, be a source of food and conserve the environment.
  • Conducting waste management and cleanup activities while encouraging the 3Rs.
  • It also targets schools so as to spread more environmental awareness and encourage the children to be innovative.

Objectives

  • To engage the women in the community and empower them through sustainable projects and knowledge.
  • To actively spread environmental consciousness and climate change awareness
  • To mitigate and adapt to the impacts of climate change
  • To effectively manage our waste and ensure a cleaner town and oceans
  • To work together and partner with other likeminded groups for better innovative ideas

Vision

To ensure a clean, healthy, safe and sustainably managed environment and its resources.

Mission

To empower the women in the community through innovative projects and engaging them in the conservation activities for better livelihoods. To change the community patterns towards climate awareness and environmental consciousness to create a more sustainable and resilient environment.
